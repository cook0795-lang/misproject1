Professional Introduction

Hello! I'm James Cook, a dedicated student at the University of Minnesota Duluth pursuing my passion for technology and innovation. My journey in higher education has been driven by a desire to learn useful skills to apply later in life.

I'm currently focused on developing my skills in software development, data analysis, and project management. My academic experience at UMD has provided me with a foundation in both technical and analytical thinking, preparing me for the challenges of the modern workplace.

Beyond academics, I'm passionate about continuous learning and staying current with emerging technologies. I believe in the power of collaboration and am always eager to work with others to solve complex problems and create innovative solutions.

Hobby Highlights

Online Market Investing

My passion with online markets started as a curiosity and has evolved into one of my greatest passions. What began with simple micro transactions for video games has grown into a deep understanding of multiple micro economies both inside and outside of video games.

Conquering The Wilderness

Living in Duluth has given me access to some of the most beautiful natural landscapes in Minnesota. Hiking and outdoor activities have become essential for maintaining balance in my life, especially during the demanding academic semesters.
