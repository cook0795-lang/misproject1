Hotdog vs Condiments

Defend your hotdog from the condiment invasion!

Score 0
Level 1

How to Play:

  • Use Left and Right Arrow keys to move the hotdog
  • Press S to shoot condiment bottles (ketchup and mustard)
  • Destroy all condiment bottles in each level
  • Don't let them reach the bottom of the screen
  • Click "Next Level" to continue when you clear a level
  • Each level gets 2.5% faster!
  • Use touch controls below for mobile play
  • If the game freezes, refresh the page to continue playing
